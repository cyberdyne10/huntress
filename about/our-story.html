Our Story

We built CYBER-LOGIC NETWORKS because we believed small and mid-market businesses deserved the same level of protection as the Fortune 500.

Founded by former NSA Cyber Operators

Our founders spent years at the NSA, breaking into networks to understand how attackers operate. They realized that the tools they were using weren't available to the businesses that needed them most. So, they built Cyber-Logic to democratize cybersecurity.
